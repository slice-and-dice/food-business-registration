Questions about charity numbers

By law, if you set up a charity you must have applied to register it with the commission if it is a charitable incorporated organisation (CIO) or its annual income is more than £5,000, unless it is a specific type of charity that doesn’t have to register. The commission will take action to secure compliance if it identifies a charity which isn’t registered but should be.

If your charity isn’t a CIO and your income is under £5,000 you don’t have to register with the commission. You can find your charity reference number by visiting the Charity register.
